Software Testing

Manual Testing: I provide comprehensive manual testing services to ensure the quality and reliability of your software applications. This includes creating test plans, test cases, executing tests, and reporting defects. I have expertise in functional, regression, integration, and user acceptance testing. Test Case Design: I can help you design effective and efficient test cases that cover various scenarios, ensuring maximum test coverage and identifying potential issues early in the development lifecycle. Defect Management: I offer defect management services, which involve identifying, documenting, prioritizing, and tracking software defects throughout the testing process. I can work closely with your development team to ensure timely resolution of issues. Test Execution: I conduct thorough test execution to validate the functionality of your software applications, ensuring they meet the specified requirements. I meticulously analyze test results, provide comprehensive reports, and work with your team to address any identified issues. Test Documentation: I create detailed documentation, including test plans, test cases, and test summary reports, to provide clear and concise information about the testing process, results, and recommendations for improvement.

  • Test Framework Development: I specialize in developing robust and scalable test automation frameworks tailored to your specific software testing needs.
